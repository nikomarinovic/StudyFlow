Frequently asked questions

Is the free plan really free?
Yes, completely. The Free plan includes the core features you need to build great study habits — no credit card required, ever.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Absolutely. You can cancel your Pro or Team plan at any time. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period with no questions asked.
What is the AI study coach?
The AI coach analyzes your study patterns, suggests optimal session lengths, gives personalized tips based on your upcoming exams, and adjusts your schedule when you fall behind.
Does my data sync across devices?
Currently StudyFlow saves data locally on your device. Cross-device sync is in active development and will be rolling out soon — it's one of our top priorities!
